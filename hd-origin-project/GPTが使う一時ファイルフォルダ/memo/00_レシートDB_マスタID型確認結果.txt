[確認目的]
新しく作るレシート系テーブルの *_id 型を、既存マスタ側のID型と揃えるため。

[psql結果]
 table_schema |   table_name    |    column_name    | data_type | udt_name 
--------------+-----------------+-------------------+-----------+----------
 expenses     | account_titles  | account_title_id  | bigint    | int8
 expenses     | departments     | department_id     | bigint    | int8
 expenses     | evidence_types  | evidence_type_id  | bigint    | int8
 expenses     | invoice_types   | invoice_type_id   | bigint    | int8
 expenses     | payment_methods | payment_method_id | bigint    | int8
 expenses     | projects        | project_id        | bigint    | int8
 expenses     | purposes        | purpose_id        | bigint    | int8
 expenses     | target_people   | target_person_id  | bigint    | int8
 expenses     | tax_categories  | tax_category_id   | bigint    | int8
 expenses     | tax_treatments  | tax_treatment_id  | bigint    | int8
(10 行)


[見方]
data_type が integer なら、新テーブル側も INTEGER にする。
data_type が bigint なら、新テーブル側も BIGINT にする。
ここで既存マスタIDの型に合わせる。
